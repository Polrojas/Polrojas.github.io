Leading from within

Acknowledging and releasing your potential

Leadership inspires performance. How can you continue improving your ability to motivate groups and individuals to achieve tangible results?

A key factor in successful leadership lies in your ability to know yourself and be aware of the impact that your behaviors and attitudes have on your team and other key stakeholders. By knowing your patterns, empathizing with others, and being aware of your environment, you enhance your sense of responsibility (the ability to respond). The goal of this leadership retreat is to uncover and release your innate potential by examining the following basic human principles of being a leader:

  • Our personality (values, preferences, and temperament)
  • What drives us to act (motivation)
  • Our capacity to influence others (persuasive ability)
  • Our emotional intelligence (self regulation, social skills, empathy)
  • How and with whom we communicate (information sharing)
  • How we deal with conflict (fight - freeze - flight)

    • In an increasingly complex and rapidly-moving world, grounding and connecting to self is essential in order to build leadership capacities. Slowing down fosters resilience and allows for multiple perspectives to be considered when confronted with complexity.

    Why I should attend

    With practical tools, coping skills, techniques, and inner knowledge you will leave with greater appreciation of yourself and insight into your next developmental step towards becoming the leader you are meant to be. Fun and curiosity will lead you to new perspectives and widen your personal and professional horizons.

    We all have the resources to motivate and inspire others. Working collectively on leadership skills can trigger feelings, thoughts, and behaviors that sit more deeply within than we realize. Practice and patience are needed to allow new ‘modus operandi’ to become habits. This retreat is a significant step in supporting you on your journey.

    Who would most benefit from this

    Team leaders, managers, entrepreneurs, project managers, people in leadership roles regardless of organizations, as well as any person committed to becoming a more inspirational leader, a catalyst for change.

    If you feel inspired by the retreat, but are not sure if it is for you, get in touch with one of our hosts.
    We will be happy to talk to you to provide more clarity around your decision.

    Why Rhodes and Greece?

    “Everything in Greece, mountains, rivers, seas, valleys get humanised… and speak to the human in an almost human language. They don’t cover or smash him/her, they don’t suppress they become friends and collaborators. The blurry, bending cry when transiting the light of Greece gets crystallised, humanised it becomes logos…

    Greece is the filter that sculptures the animal into human, the slavery into freedom and the drunkness into sober pondering…Giving person to the impersonal, measure to the immeasurable…balancing the conflicting blind forces… this is the mission of the much-tortured land and sea that is called Greece.”     ~ Nikos Kazantzakis, Greek Author

    The words above by Nikos Kazantzakis, although written a few decades ago, reflect the timeless quality of Greece, that of friendliness, hospitality, living and embracing the human scale, honouring the human. The land herself and Rhodes as well consists of a geographical and psychological crossroad between East and West and as such hospitality, embrace of diversity and reconciliation has been a core part of her journey in history. Rhodes has literally been a place for hosting pilgrims of the three main religions of the Middle East, a place where this meeting has been natural, warm and fully embraced. It is this hospitality of diversity, an essential quality and principle of our framework and the ancient core value and principle of “Philoxenia”, being friendly to the guest that inspires and stimulates our gathering for this Global Leadership Retreat.

    Fondation Marc de Montalembert

    The venue that will be hosting us for this Leadership Retreat is a rather unique and special place. A beautiful spot in the Medieval Town of Rhodes, where all cultures from the ancient Greek, hellenistic to the medieval and ottoman are present offering an experiential touch of the multicultural DNA and mark of Rhodes.

    The Foundation is dedicated in the memory of Marc de Montalembert and his vision of a peaceful world and Mediterranean Sea regions rich in cross-cultural dialogue and understanding. It offers its support to young people as well as its premises and unique space for gatherings of diversity for unity, dialogue, human evolution, creativity and peace. It is a space protected by noise and hectic movement by its physical position in the old town, architecture and care. With a big open space, the ottoman “hayat” appropriate for the plenary meetings and smaller open-air and in-house artfully created corners for smaller group conversations and activities, the Foundation provides ideal and at the same time very special facilities, space, inspiration and atmosphere, for this international retreat. The Foundation’s jewel though is its garden: the home of native plants, herbs, trees and flowers from the Mediterranean region it blossoms in full white offering a gift for the eyes, the whole body and the soul. It is a place for serenity, connection and wholeness supporting our personal and collective leadership.

    Leadership Teams Development

    What we do

    We put together a diagnostic of the team environment and effectiveness, through existing elements and/or different proven methodologies, focused on a diverse spectrum of aspects, like communication, leadership, teamwork, decision-making, among others. We also support the team in the design of action plans that positively impact their leadership and environment, thus channeling their energy in those key strong areas to continue to build on and those to be improved upon.

    Deliverable

    A report that reflects the current situation in the organization or team, displaying strengths and areas of opportunity regarding different aspects of the team’s experience. We then have a team session to go over the findings and help the team put together an action plan which will then have a positive impact in the team’s climate, their productivity and the organization’s performance.

    Benefits

    Knowing the current state of affairs in terms of the team’s effectiveness, which allows them to identify the priority areas on which to base an effective action plan. Therefore, the team focuses on those actions that will positively impact the organization, and minimizes investment in those that do not require priority focus. At the same time, from the employee standpoint, the feeling of being heard and considered as a change agent increases engagement and loyalty towards the organization.

    Desarrollo de Tableros de Control

    En la actualidad las empresas utilizan los tableros de control o ‘dashboards’ para llevar a cabo procesos de gestión decisivos como son:

  • Clarificar y traducir la visión y la estrategia
  • Comunicar y vincular los objetivos e indicadores estratégicos
  • Establecer objetivos y medir su cumplimiento
  • Medir los resultados de las iniciativas

    • Los mismos deben medir las siguientes perspectivas: Financiera, Del Cliente, Procesos Internos, Aprendizaje y Crecimiento.

    Estos permiten equilibrar los resultados a corto y a largo plazo y alinearlos a la estrategia. Sin embargo, un buen tablero de control no sólo debe incluir medidas de resultados sino también de indicadores de desempeño (drivers).

    Los tableros pueden ser: Operativos (para el seguimiento diario de un sector o proceso), Directivos (para monitorear los resultados de las áreas clave) y/o Estratégicos (si se quiere medir la evolución de la empresa en el mercado).

    La buena noticia es que para confeccionar un tablero no se necesita una inversión significativa en software especializado.

    En Insights Beacon te podemos ayudar en:

  • Identificar las estrategias de tu empresa
  • Definir los objetivos y metas
  • Determinar los indicadores de desempeño
  • Diseñar un tablero en un formato amigable a las necesidades de cada cliente

    La experiencia MBTI

    Basado en las Teorías de Personalidad de Carl Jung, el Indicador de Tipo MyersBriggs (MBTI®) es la herramienta de personalidad más confiable y respetada en el mundo con más de 70 años de investigación y desarrollo, y se administra anualmente a más de cuatro millones de personas en el mundo.

    Entender mis preferencias y cómo ellas impactan mis acciones, elecciones, relacionamiento y toma de decisiones, entre otras, así también como el entendimiento de las preferencias de los demás, me ayudará a mejorar mi comunicación y la forma de llegar al otro.

    No existen tipologías buenas ni malas, cada Tipo tiene sus fortalezas y sus puntos ciegos. Estar consciente de ambos, me servirán para ser más efectivo.

    El MBTI ayuda a equipos y sus integrantes a entender sus diferencias, y a utilizar esa diversidad para mejorar la comunicación, la consecución de objetivos, la toma de decisiones más efectivas, los estilos de liderazgo, la resolución de conflictos, el manejo de relaciones y del cambio, y los disparadores de stress.

    Se administra a través de un test online y una sesión de aprendizaje y aplicación con un facilitador certificado.

    Desde Insights Beacon, te ofrecemos ‘La experiencia MBTI’, que incluye:

  • La administración del Indicador, online, en forma individual o para un grupo o equipo.
  • Una sesión de aprendizaje sobre tu Tipo según tus preferencias, y las de tu equipo/grupo, si fuera grupal.
  • Una sesión de aplicación del Tipo a la toma de decisiones, la comunicación y planificación, y los posibles disparadores de stress. En una sesión grupal, también se harán dinámicas para entender las fortalezas y posibles ‘puntos ciegos’ del equipo, así como la optimización de la toma de decisiones, comunicación y expectativas.
  • Sesiones de coaching individual, o grupal, para trabajar aquellas áreas en las que el cliente quiere profundizar.
